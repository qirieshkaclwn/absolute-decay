Version: 0.1.0
  Date: 28. 06. 2026
  Features:
    - Initial release.
    - Added absolute spoilage for all crafted items to turn into their most expensive solid ingredient.
    - Added settings to customize spoilage time (default is 30 minutes).
    - Blacklisted basic resources (ores, coal, stone, wood, asteroid chunks, etc.) from spoiling into ingredients; they spoil into standard spoilage instead.
    - Excluded blueprints, planners, and copy-paste tools from spoilage to avoid GUI issues.
    - Excluded recycling and unbarrelling recipes from ingredient cost calculations.
